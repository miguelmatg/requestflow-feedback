# Code of Conduct

This community is focused on respectful, productive feedback.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on the problem, not the person.
- Assume good intent.
- Keep reports clear and actionable.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or hate speech.
- Personal attacks or insulting language.
- Spam or off-topic abuse.
- Sharing private or sensitive information.

## Moderation

Maintainers may edit, lock, or remove content that violates this policy.
Repeated violations may lead to account restrictions in this repository.
